Why Screening?

Your safety and mine are my top priorities. The screening process is completely confidential and helps ensure we're both comfortable and compatible before meeting.

All information is encrypted and stored securely. I never share client details with anyone.

Verification Process

1

Initial Contact

Reach out via email or contact form with your preferred meeting details and basic introduction.

2

Verification

Provide verification information using one of the approved methods listed below.

3

Confirmation

Once verified, I'll confirm our appointment with time, location, and any special requests.

Verification Methods

Please choose ONE of the following verification methods:

Employment Verification

Professional verification through your workplace (most common method):

  • • Work email address and phone number
  • • LinkedIn profile (preferred)
  • • Business card or company website reference

Provider References

References from 2 other verified providers you've seen recently:

  • • Provider names and contact information
  • • Approximate dates of your meetings
  • • Permission to contact them for reference

Identity Verification

Direct identity verification (for new clients):

  • • Photo ID showing name and age
  • • Real phone number for brief verification call
  • • May require additional social media verification

Alternative Methods

Other acceptable verification methods:

  • • Membership in verified client databases
  • • Professional association memberships
  • • Contact me to discuss other options

Your Privacy is Protected

Confidentiality

All client information is kept strictly confidential. I never discuss clients with anyone, including other providers, except for safety verification purposes.

Data Security

All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. Information is automatically deleted after a reasonable period unless you become a regular client.

No Judgments

Everyone deserves respect and discretion. Your personal life, relationship status, and reasons for seeking companionship are completely private.

Mutual Respect

The screening process is about creating a foundation of trust and safety. I appreciate your understanding and cooperation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Most verifications are completed within 2-6 hours during business hours. I strive to respond as quickly as possible.

What if I can't provide the requested information?

Please contact me to discuss alternative verification methods. I'm flexible and want to find a solution that works for both of us.

Do I need to verify for every appointment?

No, once you're verified as a client, future bookings are much simpler. Just reference your previous verification when booking.

Is my information really safe?

Absolutely. I understand the importance of discretion and privacy. Your information is encrypted and handled with the utmost care.

Ready to Begin?

I understand the screening process can seem daunting, but it's designed to be as simple and discreet as possible. I'm here to help if you have any questions.
