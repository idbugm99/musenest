Etiquette & Guidelines

Professional standards for our engagement

A Refined Experience

I believe that every encounter should be elegant, respectful, and memorable. These guidelines ensure we both have the most wonderful time together.

Booking Etiquette

Advance Notice

Please provide at least 24 hours notice for appointments. Last-minute requests may incur an additional fee.

Screening Process

All clients must complete our discrete screening process. This ensures safety and quality for everyone.

Payment

Payment is expected at the beginning of our time together. Cash is preferred, with other methods available.

Cancellations

Cancellations within 2 hours of appointment time may incur a 50% fee. No-shows are charged in full.

Meeting Guidelines

Personal Hygiene

Please arrive freshly showered and well-groomed. Breath mints and cologne/perfume should be subtle. Shower facilities are available if needed.

Dress Code

Business casual or formal attire is appreciated for dinner dates. For private encounters, clean and presentable clothing is expected.

Communication

Respectful communication is essential. Please keep conversations pleasant and avoid inappropriate topics unless specifically discussed beforehand.

Boundaries & Respect

Important Notice

Consent and mutual respect are paramount. Any behavior that makes me uncomfortable will result in immediate termination of our time together, with no refund.

What's Welcome

  • Respectful conversation and genuine connection
  • Clear communication about preferences
  • Appreciation and gentlemanly behavior
  • Flexibility and understanding

Not Acceptable

  • Aggressive or disrespectful behavior
  • Substance abuse during our time
  • Pushing boundaries or ignoring limits
  • Recording or photography without consent
